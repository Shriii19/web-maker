Getting Started Tutorial

1 Select a Template

Browse through our collection of professionally designed templates specifically created for trucking communities. Choose one that fits your brand and needs.

Use our intuitive editor to modify colors, content, layout, and styling. Add components like job lists, driver profiles, and maps to enhance your site.

How TruckCraft Studio Works

TruckCraft Studio helps VTC communities create professional websites without coding knowledge. Our platform simplifies the process into three easy steps:

Template Selection

Choose from our library of responsive, trucking-specific templates designed by industry professionals.

Visual Customization

Customize every aspect of your template using our intuitive editor - colors, fonts, layout, and content.

Export & Deploy

Download your finished template as clean HTML/CSS code ready for immediate implementation on your site.

Continuous Updates

All templates are regularly updated with new features and components to keep your site modern and functional.
