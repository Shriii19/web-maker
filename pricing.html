Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing plans

💳 Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and we'll prorate any charges.

💰 Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans, no questions asked. You can cancel anytime.

No setup fees, ever. You only pay the monthly subscription fee. Get started immediately with any plan.

🌐 Can I use my own domain?

Yes, Pro and Enterprise plans include custom domain support with easy one-click setup and SSL certificates.

📊 What's included in analytics?

Track visitor statistics, page views, traffic sources, and user engagement with our comprehensive analytics dashboard.

🛠️ Do I need technical skills?

Not at all! Our drag-and-drop builder is designed for everyone. No coding knowledge required, but code editing is available for advanced users.
